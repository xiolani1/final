Evolution of Art

In this artistic project, I have intentionally introduced glitches to a series of well-known images from the realm of classical and historical art. The objective is to explore and establish new layers of meaning by juxtaposing traditional artworks with the disruptive aesthetics of new media art. Each image within the series undergoes a unique glitching process, resulting in various transformations. These glitches range from altering the texture of the image to emphasizing significant elements within the artwork, thereby magnifying their impact and creating a compelling visual experience.
