Artworks

"Ivan the Terrible and His Son Ivan" is a powerful painting that captures a poignant moment in history. Painted by Ilya Repin in 1885, it portrays Tsar Ivan IV, also known as Ivan the Terrible, grieving over the body of his son. The painting's composition and use of light evoke a sense of sorrow and despair, emphasizing the emotional turmoil within the scene. Repin's attention to detail and the expressive facial expressions of the characters contribute to the profound narrative, inviting viewers to reflect on the complexities of power, family, and the human condition.
